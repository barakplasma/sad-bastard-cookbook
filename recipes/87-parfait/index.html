  • Parfait

Core Ingredients

  • Yogurt

  • Store-bought cookies (or biscuits if you’re Irish)

  • Jam

Preparation

  • Add yogurt and jam to your bowl.

  • Crumble up cookies on top.

  • Eat with a spoon.

Variations

  • Defrost frozen fruit in the microwave and use that instead of jam.

  • Or use fresh fruit.

Our professional-chef friend informed us this is closer to an Eton Mess than to a parfait, but really, isn’t everything that comes out of Eton a disastrous mess?
